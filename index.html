About Me

My name is Elena Moreau, and I'm a photographer based in Paris. For me, photography isn't just about taking pictures; it's about freezing a moment in time, capturing the raw emotion, and telling a story that words cannot.

With over a decade of experience, I've honed my craft to find the extraordinary in the ordinary. Whether it's the intimate glance between a newly married couple or the majestic silence of a mountain peak at dawn, I strive to create images that are both timeless and evocative.
